Collaborative Suturing using Reinforcement Learning

Come check our paper presentation at RO-MAN 2020!

Performed an exploration of a discrete reinforcement learning-based approach to automate the needle hand-off task. Users were asked to perform a simple running suture using the da Vinci Research Kit. The user trajectory was learnt by generating a sparse reward function and deriving an optimal policy using Q-learning.
